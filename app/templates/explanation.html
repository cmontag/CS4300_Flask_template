our process

Pick Your Poison is your answer to all questions alcohol. We combine a powerful recommendation engine with an extensive drink database to serve you your top choice of any kind of drink. You'll be able to read reviews from professional sommeliers and cicerones, look for specific flavors and prices, and order the drinks you like the most for home delivery.

We know firsthand that it can be a challenge finding the right words to describe your favorite drink, not to mention that the language used by drink vendors and reviewers can vary wildly! That's why we tailor our searches to a list of about 600 descriptors specific to the drink industry to help you streamline the way you look for drinks. Originating from contributions by researchers and drink experts representing places like UCA, UC Davis, and Wine Folly, this list took the form of tiered wheels useful for standardizing and categorizing drink jargon into a usable form.

Our ranking process is based off of the descriptions and reviews of every drink in our database. We specifically extract descriptors from the outermost tier of our wheels, the ones you can search with, for as precise a ranking as possible. By training a machine learning model on thousands of these texts and building a vector space for all of our drinks, we've enabled you to find unique matches for the descriptors you're most interested in at instant speed.

We hope you've found this insight into our process helpful. Now go practice some safe drinking!
